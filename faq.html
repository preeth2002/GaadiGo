FAQs

RENTERS

What all documents are required for verification?

To rent a car, you should have a valid Aadhaar card, a driving license and an original Passport/Voters ID card.
Please note: Aadhaar card details like name, mobile number etc. should match the details that are provided to GaadiGo at time of making a booking. In case of a mismatch, Avis will cancel the booking and a penalty of Rs 1000 will be levied to the guest in such case.

What is the fuel policy?

As per the GaadiGo fuel policy:

  • The car will be delivered with a full tank and need to be returned in the same manner.
  • Customer pays for the fuel charges based on the usage.
  • If the car is returned with less than full tank fuel, Avis will charge for the differential fuel.
  • The assessment made by the GaadiGo executive on the differential fuel amount will be final.

What is the cancellation policy?

As per GadiGo cancellation policy, if booking is cancelled:

Cancellation Done Deduction on Total Rental Amount
Prior to 48 hours No cancellation charge
24-48 from rental time 3%
4-24 hours from rental time 50%
0-4 hours from rental time 100%
No show 100%

USERS

What are the requirements for listing a vehicle?

You must be above 18 years old and have the necessary vehicle documents and insurance to list a car.
The car must also not be more than 12 years old and should not have run more than 20,000 kilometres.

How do I contact customers?

All communication between you and your customer must take place through GaadiGo messaging.
You can message a customer only after they have requested or booked a trip in one of your vehicles.

How can I claim reimbursement for damages to my vehicle?

If your vehicle is returned damaged, you have two options. You could either directly approach the renter for reimbursement for the damages. Alternatively, you could file a complaint with GaadiGo and we will take further action after thorough evaluation.
